Best Movies Of 2023

The Main Contenders

Attack On Titan The Final Season: Part 2

This movie was the most epic and dark conclusion to the
most epic anime of all time! Full of twists and turns and insane
action, this conclusion was well worth the wait!

Super Mario Bros Movie

This is very much a biased view as this is only warranted for
those who are avid fans of the super mario bros video
game franchise! Full of easter eggs and a very feel-good watch
for the whole family.

Saw X

To some this may be a hot take, but Saw X was one of the best
entries in the franchise! A refreshing take on the series and
the return of Tobin Bell makes this horror experience a must-see!
